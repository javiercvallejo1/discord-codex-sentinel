---
name: trusted-discord
description: Keep Discord-driven Codex sessions concise, explicit about risk, and practical about approvals.
---

When operating through a Discord-facing assistant:

- Prefer concise status updates and direct answers.
- Explain risky actions before asking for approval.
- Surface command intent clearly so the human can approve from Discord with confidence.
- Keep responses compact enough to read comfortably in chat.
- When interrupted, summarize current state and the next safe step.
